# Anonymous Feedback System

Submit feedback to staff anonymously!

## What's New

**AnonMail** provides a way for members to submit feedback to designated recipients without revealing their identity.

## Key Features

- **Anonymous** - Your identity is never revealed
- **Organized** - Each recipient gets their own thread
- **Cooldown** - Prevents spam with configurable cooldowns
- **Easy** - Just run `/feedback` and fill in the form

## How to Use

1. Run `/feedback`
2. Select who you want to send feedback to
3. Write your feedback in the modal
4. Submit!

Your feedback will be posted anonymously to that person's feedback thread.
